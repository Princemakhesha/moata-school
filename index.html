What we do

pan_tool

Hands-on training

Moata School offers hands-on equipment training for excavation and construction equipment operators looking to get employed in the forestry, mining, civil, road building, and oil and gas sectors. Since 2010, we have strived to provide practical hands-on equipment training.

school

Wide variety of courses

We offer a wide variety of training courses to better convenience our learners and get you the certification you need in different industry programs – from Machinery Training Courses to Safety compliance courses, and other professional courses.

group

Dedicated Team

We go to great lengths to identify self-starters with diverse experience and skill sets to produce not just an effective workforce, but a well-rounded, talented and complementary team that works across its Training Courses offered to support our work investing in learners.

We recognise the need for skills training and improvement as a tool of empowerment and personal upliftment for the economically disadvantaged populace of South Africa.

Development Opportunities

Our team structure enables you to grow as a professional. Our ultimate goal: develop your skills and talents in line with your interests and career goals.

Personal Support & Engagement

We understand that a learner can’t be productive without a healthy and multifacet environment.

Our support spans far beyond the institute doors. Not only do we provide whole life changing advice to our learners for creativity, but we also engage them through frequent service opportunities.
